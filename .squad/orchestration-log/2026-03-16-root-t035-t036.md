## T036 — Dashboard.razor idle wiring

- `TauriEventService.OnIdleDetected` subscribed in `OnInitializedAsync`, unsubscribed in `Dispose()`
- Handler guards on `payload.HadActiveTimer` — no modal when no active timer (matches spec + Root decision)
- `InvokeAsync` wraps `Show()` + `StateHasChanged()` — Blazor thread safety for events from non-Blazor threads
- `idle_ended_at` captured at `Show()` time (`DateTime.UtcNow.ToString("o")`) — not at button-click time (avoids drift)
- `OnResolved` callback triggers `RefreshList()` → `TimeEntryList.LoadPage(1)`
